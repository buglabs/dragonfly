Download Application to BUG

  1. In Eclipse, in the BUGnet panel, click on an application's link.
  2. If necessary, log in with your BUGnet username and password.
  3. On the application's view, click Download Latest Version to SDK. Eclipse downloads and installs the application.
  4. Launch your BUG.
  5. Right click on the application in the Project Explorer pane.
  6. Select Send to BUG. The application is now on your BUG.
