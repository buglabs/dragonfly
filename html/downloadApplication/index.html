Download an Application from BUGnet

To download an application from BUGnet to the Dragonfly SDK:

  1. In Eclipse, switch to the Dragonfly perspective.
  2. In the BUGnet view, in the Bug Apps list, right click on an application's link.
  3. Select Import To Dragonfly SDK

Alternatively,

  1. In the BUGnet view click on an application's link.
  2. If necessary, log in with your BUGnet username and password.
  3. On the application's view, click Download Latest Version to SDK.
    Eclipse downloads and installs the application.
